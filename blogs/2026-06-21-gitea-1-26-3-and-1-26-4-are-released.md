Gitea 1.26.3 and 1.26.4 have been released. Version 1.26.3 delivers a large set of security fixes alongside important bug fixes and stability improvements, and 1.26.4 follows immediately with a fix for a repository code-page regression introduced in 1.26.3 plus an additional security fix.
